### Minor New Features:
• New features have been added to the Mechanical Armory:
  • You may now store ability keybinds under the class section
of a loadout
  • You may now store cosmetic skills under the class section of
a loadout
  • You may now retrieve any item from within your equipment
cases via using the swap key while hovering over the retrieve
items button
  • You may now set Depths/Zenith tree preferences by right
clicking on the glass pane next to the set class button

### Minor New Features:
• Damage Splashes and Health Displays for enemies have been
added as a new option inside the P.E.B's "Gameplay Options"
  • Damage Splashes can be configured to show only the damage
number or the damage number with damage types
  • Enemy health displays can be viewed from 3.5 blocks away
initially, then from 64 blocks away (not through blocks) after
the mob is damaged. They can be configured to hide the mob name,
showing "ʙᴏss" or "ᴇʟɪᴛᴇ" tags instead. Completely invisible
enemies do not show the health display.

no longer have an extra 0.25s of invisibility
