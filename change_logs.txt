### Sporous Amalgam
• A new quarry has been spotted in the Ring! Meet veteran hunter
Berteau in the Diamenean Lodge to learn about the Sporous
Amalgam and its unique mechanics.
• Sporous Amalgam will be added into the pool for Hunts quarries
right after the update and for the next week, Sporous Amalgam
will be hunted if the previous quarry hunted was not Sporous
Amalgam.
• Other than that, Sporous Amalgam will be treated the same as
other Diamenean Hunts bosses: new items are available to
purchase from Tiinah using the loot from the boss, and players
who did not already complete all quarries unspoiled will need to
complete Sporous Amalgam unspoiled in order to unlock the epic
and infusion (players who already had access will not have it
revoked).
