### Rebound:
• Rebound has been reworked:
  • Rebound: Upon shield stun, enemies within 4 blocks of you
are knocked away and take (level &o 5) damage. Aspect
enchantments, Decay, Bleed, and Impact are also applied.
Knockback levels increase the force of the rebound.
  • Additionally, Rebound's knockback has been reworked to be in
the direction you are facing, akin to Aeroblast.
