🏓Hey everyone! After more than five years serving as a project
lead for Monumenta, I've decided it's time to step down and
focus on other interests and areas of life. It's been a truly
incredible journey and one that has been immensely meaningful
for me. The server has grown leaps and bounds since I joined-• I
remember having neighbors on betaplots or even a tiny apartment
in Sierhaven to hold my limited belongings; times when the
online player count could be shown on one hand. When R2 released
in 2019, I had just graduated college and was eager to
contribute and give back to the game. After learning the ropes
and leading my first full project, Echoes of Oblivion, I was
asked to be a lead in 2020.
Years later, I'm proud to say almost everyone here has played
something I had a hand in, whether that be Teal, Depths/Zenith,
the PORTAL series or just enjoying some Seasonal Pass missions.
While I didn't do everything perfectly (and still don't), it
warmed my heart seeing the reception to these projects and
watching players get to experience them. There's nothing like
the anticipation and excitement of spectating day one of your
content launch, and it's great getting to see our newer
developers sharing this experience these days. I still love
Monumenta and always will, so I'll be back to play the new
updates and content as long as the team is around and kicking.
Rest assured, it's in good hands.
Over the years I have had the privilege of leading a fantastic
team of volunteer developers, defying expectations of what is
possible every year. I've had hundreds of genuine and
constructive interactions with passionate players, and learned a
huge amount about game design and development while pushing the
boundaries of what gameplay looks like in Monumenta. Like many
of you all, I'm fortunate to have met some awesome friends from
this wonderful community, ones that I expect to have for years
to come. There's so much more than could fit in a simple goodbye
message, but all of it meant the world to me.

on Monumenta.## Announcing the Official Monumenta Modpack
Hello everyone, I am pleased to announce the creation of the
Official Monumenta Modpack. Throughout the years, many players
have expressed frustration and confusion on how difficult it can
be to set up the Monumenta resource packs, find useful
modifications, or improve the Monumenta experience. The modpack
aims to solve these issues by providing new and veteran players
alike with a developer-curated set of modifications that work
without extensive manual configuration and troubleshooting.
&bThis modpack is not a requirement to join or play on
Monumenta.&r To set up the modpack, download a third-party
launcher such as the [Modrinth App](<https://modrinth.com/app>)
or [Prism Launcher](<https://prismlauncher.org/>) and grab a
copy of the modpack from the Modrinth page:
https://modrinth.com/modpack/official-monumenta-modpack
### Additional Notes
Everyone here on Team Monumenta wishes to stress this modpack is
not intended to take the place of community-made mods or
modpacks that many dedicated players have set up. This modpack
would not exist were it not for the inspiration we took from
many of those modpacks. With that said, we are in the process of
updating in-game links and information to point to it. Please
let us know if there are places we missed after the weekly
update.
Some eagle-eyed scouts reading through the official modpack's
Modrinth page may recognize it looks familiar to a previous
modpack I created called Spy's Lightweight Monumenta. The
official modpack is a rebrand of that project. When the team was
discussing setting up an official modpack, I offered it up as
its ethos of being lightweight with minimal setup time matched
the official modpack's needs. Truly, I am thankful to everyone
who used it throughout these last few years as I didn't expect
it to become one of the main community-recommended modpacks. I
hope the next phase of its life serves you all just as well.###
Coalrupted Sierhaven
The King's Valley has been blasted with a sudden polar vortex
this spring! Enjoy a snow-covered Sierhaven as the Winter event
returns for a limited time again!
Coalrupted Sierhaven is a new gamemode in the King's Valley.
Enjoy fast-paced, strike-like gameplay while exploring a giant,
open map with many secrets within. Gather coal from chests and
deposit it while fighting against the clock. As you play, you'll
unlock more and more Snow Perks that will open up new avenues of
enjoying the content!
Coalrupted Sierhaven will be available
&bpermanently&r, even after the event ends! You can find
Coalrupted Sierhaven at the Snow Spirit lobby, and completion of
Snow Man Left Behind is required. Happy coal collecting!
