&bA Decade of Monumenta:&r
On May 28th, 2016, a Discord server was created for the
development of what was, at the time, known as Project Epic.
Over the next year, this group worked on builds, quests, and
items, eventually leading into the announcement of the project a
year later, and what it was called: Monumenta. A server that
took the genre of Complete the Monument adventure maps, and
converted it into a MMO experience. The founding of this
original discord marks the start of Monumenta's development •
and therefore, the server has now been in existence
&bfor an entire decade!&r 🎉
Without all of you, our community, we would not be in this
position today. The developer team has changed heavily over the
past decade, with members joining and leaving constantly, but
every single one of us had one goal in mind •
&bto create an experience worth playing&r... and it has been so
amazing seeing how far we've come over the years.
Starting with this weekly update, we have a couple of activities
and gifts for you to experience during our 10th Anniversary
Event, which will last 3-4 weeks. Come celebrate with us in-game
over the next few weeks!
One of our oldest developers also has some of their own thoughts
to share regarding their time developing for the server, which
will follow this message. Thank you for ten years, and here's to
many more!

Applications
Hello Monumenta! A friendly reminder that moderator applications
are always open and we'd always love the help!
We'd like to encourage anyone qualified, willing, and interested
in being a moderator to apply!
&bREQUIREMENTS&r
• Have a good reputation in the community and be known to the
existing mods as trustworthy, honest, fair, helpful, and mature.
• Have a consistent history of activity in the community,
&bboth in-game and in Discord&r.
• Completion of most major content in Monumenta (dungeons,
strikes, bosses, and quests).
• Proficiency in English.
• At least 16 years of age.
&bPreferred but Optional&r (be sure to mention these in your
application if relevant)
• Being active at a time when most existing moderators are
unavailable. Being in a non-American/Canadian time zone helps.
• Being extremely familiar with Monumenta's ins-and-outs. The
more expertise, the better.
• Proficiency in a non-English language.
• Previous experience moderating an online community, or with MC
commands/servers.
&bResponsibilities&r
• Helping players in-game with technical issues.
• Making judgment calls about "gray areas" in applying the
rules.
• Being willing to be 🏓 'd here in Discord and in-game to help
players.
• Being online and in-game relatively frequently on an average
week.
• Being fair, polite, and patient in dealing with problems.
&bPerks&r
• Beta Test unreleased content and limited access to the Team
Monumenta development Discord.
• Gain experience Minecraft commands in general and learn some
of how Monumenta works specifically.
• Spectate others freely.
• Work closely with dev team on various things. Many mods have
joined the dev team eventually through this path. &o(Just
saying.)&r
• Be able to fix your own problems instead of waiting on a
moderator! 😄
&bApply here!&r
