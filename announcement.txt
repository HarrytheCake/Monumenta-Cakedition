Hello everyone, I am pleased to announce the creation of the
Official Monumenta Modpack. Throughout the years, many players
have expressed frustration and confusion on how difficult it can
be to set up the Monumenta resource packs, find useful
modifications, or improve the Monumenta experience. The modpack
aims to solve these issues by providing new and veteran players
alike with a developer-curated set of modifications that work
without extensive manual configuration and troubleshooting.
&bThis modpack is not a requirement to join or play on
Monumenta.&r To set up the modpack, download a third-party
launcher such as the [Modrinth App](<https://modrinth.com/app>)
or [Prism Launcher](<https://prismlauncher.org/>) and grab a
copy of the modpack from the Modrinth page:
https://modrinth.com/modpack/official-monumenta-modpack
### Additional Notes
Everyone here on Team Monumenta wishes to stress this modpack is
not intended to take the place of community-made mods or
modpacks that many dedicated players have set up. This modpack
would not exist were it not for the inspiration we took from
many of those modpacks. With that said, we are in the process of
updating in-game links and information to point to it. Please
let us know if there are places we missed after the weekly
update.
Some eagle-eyed scouts reading through the official modpack's
Modrinth page may recognize it looks familiar to a previous
modpack I created called Spy's Lightweight Monumenta. The
official modpack is a rebrand of that project. When the team was
discussing setting up an official modpack, I offered it up as
its ethos of being lightweight with minimal setup time matched
the official modpack's needs. Truly, I am thankful to everyone
who used it throughout these last few years as I didn't expect
it to become one of the main community-recommended modpacks. I
hope the next phase of its life serves you all just as well.###
