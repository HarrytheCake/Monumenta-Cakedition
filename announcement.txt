&bZenith Anniversary Event&r
It's been two years since the Celestial Zenith launched on
December 16, 2023, and it's once again time to celebrate! Gather
your A-team and enter the dungeon with your friends between
December 10th and December 24th to participate in 6-player
Zenith!
Good luck and have fun!Rounding out October and taking us into
November, we saw the release of the major Rush of Dissonance
overhaul! Originally released back in 2020, RoD's backend
technicals and frontend gameplay mechanics had become bulky,
bloated, and a product of their time. With this fresh coat of
paint, the systems have become far more stabilized and the
gameplay refined to reduce focus on spending more and more time
fighting the same exact encounters over and over, as well as
diversify and enhance the actual space in which you play the
encounters! With it, we hope Rush now stands far more prominent
and keeps you on your toes!
Finally, we have reached December, where we have had quite the
jampacked month. Starting it off came the release of the long
awaited Alchemist, Shaman, and Berserk overhauls! Having taken
much of our class team's focus for some time, these reworks
aimed to more heavily refine and refocus the gameplay aspects
that are most enjoyed about these classes, while cutting fluff
and areas that didn't quite mesh. Following it off and wrapping
the year out, we saw the release of A Union Cross, our largest
and most ambitious quest to-date! The time has come to delve
into the latest threat arising in the dreamscape, and unlock
secrets of the past that have eluded you until now!
And with that, 2025 has come to a wrap! This has been a
phenomenal year for the game, and we didn't even have time to
discuss so many other notable releases this year like Quest
Resetting, the Friend System, Author Races, the Quest Compass
Overhaul, Skill Description Rewrites, and more Region 1
Overworld and POI overhauls than you can shake a stick at! We
have come so far this year, and we can't wait for you all to see
what we have in store for 2026!
Thank you all for taking this journey with us! We wish you all
very Merry Christmas, and a Happy New Year!In April, we took to
focusing on some backend technical aspects of the game,
specifically in regards to guilds! Not only were several QoL
features added, but after many long years, guild plots were
finally moved to their own shard/world instancing system! This
crucial shift helped us expand well beyond the spacial
limitations of the previous system, allowing us to eye an
increase in player count without any worry!
May saw the year's final dedicated boss release, the Twisted
Intruder! In one of the darkest and most twisted encounters in
the game, you are forced to face untold nightmares that lie in
worlds beyond... It can see you, but can you perceive it long
enough to stand against it?
While Monumenta's main focus in realm of new content is largely
in the Architect's Ring, it is very much our belief that all
aspects of the game should remain fun and enjoyable at all
levels. That's why with June, our balancing crew began a heavy
effort to drastically diversify the item space across both the
King's Valley and Celsian Isles. With the inclusion of
backporting several R3 enchantments and stats into the regions,
the design space for the regions has never been greater, and we
look forward to what that offers us in the coming year with the
Valley overhaul!
July needs no real introduction, as it held the launch of our
first wool dungeon in nearly years ‚Ä¢ Phyrrys, the City of Fallen
Stars! In this breathtaking dungeon, you seek out the elusive
Indigo Wool, all while in a race against time as your former
allies dart for the wool as well, and a pestering darkness
brewed on the horizon... We hope you all thorough enjoyed the
vastness of this dungeon, and look forward to this story's epic
conclusion in 2026!Something we haven't het focused on is 2025's
quiet background focus on delve modifiers, including more
experimental mods to further diversify your experience with
dungeons and POIs! With August, they really took the forefront,
with an additional five experimental mods and several overhauls,
setting the stage for more additions, adjustments, and even
replacements to come as we rounded out the year. This truly was
a year of delves focus, as we hope it all felt worth that year
spanning endeavor!
With September, the Architect's Ring experienced something that
had not occured since the release of Star Point, the launch of a
brand new POI ‚Ä¢ Fathom's Reach! Featuring a an absolutely wild
build style unlike anything Monumenta has seen prior, this
release also set the precedent for us to ship POIs to the
entrance uninhibited by the time restrictions that a full
expansion would require, allowing us to provide more gameplay
opprotunities much sooner! Something quite like it may be on the
horizon soon...
October saw the release of a long-awaited rework and replacement
to one of Monumenta more underutilized specializations, Seraph!
Functioning as a full on replacement to the previous Hierophant
skill set, Seraph sports a combo of healing and utility
playstyle unseen in other class move sets! The class development
team had been cooking this rework for some time, and we hope you
all found it worth it, though it wouldn't be the last of its
kind this year...
